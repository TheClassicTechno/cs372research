=======================================================================
4. WHAT THE JUDGE DOES
=======================================================================

The Judge is the final node after all debate rounds complete. It:

  1. Reads all agents' revised proposals + all critiques exchanged
  2. Synthesizes a single final trading decision (the actual orders)
  3. Writes an audited memo explaining why it chose this action
  4. Preserves the strongest objection — even if overruled, the best
     counter-argument is recorded in the trace for research transparency


=======================================================================
5. THE 6 SPECIALIZED AGENTS
=======================================================================

  Macro Strategist — Analyzes Fed policy, inflation, yield curves, and
    economic regime to determine how macro conditions favor or threaten
    equities

  Value/Fundamentals Analyst — Evaluates earnings, valuation multiples,
    balance sheets, and intrinsic value to identify whether stocks are
    cheap or expensive

  Risk Manager — Assesses volatility regimes, portfolio VaR, position
    sizing limits, tail risk, and correlation dynamics to flag when risk
    is too high

  Technical Analyst — Reads price action, momentum indicators,
    support/resistance levels, and volume patterns to time entries and
    exits

  Sentiment Analyst (new) — Monitors news sentiment, narrative shifts,
    information freshness, and market psychology to detect when consensus
    is shifting

  Devil's Advocate (new) — Adversarial agent that argues against the
    majority view, stress-tests assumptions, and surfaces hidden risks
    to prevent groupthink
