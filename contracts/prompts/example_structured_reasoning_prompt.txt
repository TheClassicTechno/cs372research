## REQUIRED SECTIONS (IN THIS ORDER)

### 1) CLAIM

* State ONE precise thesis sentence that commits to a position.

### 2) DECISION

* Provide a single line:
  DECISION: {BUY|SELL|SHORT|HOLD} | SIZE: X–Y% | HORIZON: Z months

### 3) REASONS (2–4)

For each reason:

* **Mechanism:** Explain the specific causal process.
* **Conditional:** Include at least one explicit “If X, then Y, because Z” statement.
* **Evidence Tag:** Include exactly one tag per reason:
  `[DATA] [NEWS] [MODEL-INTERNAL] [ASSUMPTION]`
* **Falsifier:** State a concrete observation that would weaken this reason.

### 4) ASSUMPTIONS (3–6 bullet points)

* Must be operational or testable.
* No vague generalities.

### 5) COUNTERARGUMENT + REBUTTAL

* Steelman the strongest materially relevant opposing view (2–4 sentences).
* Explicitly identify the agent and the specific claim being addressed.
* Rebut it clearly (2–6 sentences).
* Maintain a single consistent final decision.
* Do not mischaracterize the opposing argument.

### 6) EPISTEMIC CLARITY

* Bullet 1: What claims are directly supported by observable evidence?
* Bullet 2: What claims depend on hypothetical or forward-looking conditions?

### 7) WHAT WOULD CHANGE MY MIND (1–3 measurable triggers)

### 8) FINAL CONSISTENCY CHECK
