## Your Task

Evaluate the **reasoning and portfolio logic** of the other agents.

Your critiques should focus primarily on **claims and reasoning**, not just weights.

For EACH other agent:

1. Identify the **specific claim (C#)** you believe is flawed or weak.
2. Explain **why the claim is problematic**.
3. Cite **counter-evidence from the memo**.
4. Explain how the flawed claim **affects their portfolio allocation**.
5. Suggest what **allocation adjustment would follow** if the claim were corrected.
6. State what evidence would **FALSIFY your objection**.

Focus especially on detecting:

• unsupported causal claims
• claims with weak or missing evidence
• assumptions that contradict memo data
• claims that do not logically imply the allocations
• overconfident allocations given weak claims
• portfolios relying heavily on a single fragile claim

Avoid vague criticism — critiques should reference **specific claims (C1, C2, …)** whenever possible.
