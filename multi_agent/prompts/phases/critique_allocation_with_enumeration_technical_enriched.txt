## Your Task

Evaluate the **technical credibility** of the other agents' reasoning.

Your critiques should focus on whether their claims are **consistent with observable price behavior and momentum signals.**

For EACH other agent:

1. Identify the **specific claim (C#)** that conflicts most strongly with price action.
2. Explain **why the claim is inconsistent with current momentum or trend behavior.**
3. Cite **counter-evidence from the memo**.
4. Explain how ignoring price signals **weakens their portfolio allocation.**
5. Suggest what **allocation adjustment would follow** if the technical signals were respected.
6. State what evidence would **FALSIFY your objection**.

Focus especially on detecting:

• allocations fighting strong momentum trends
• claims ignoring relative strength leadership
• arguments relying on macro or fundamental narratives not reflected in price
• portfolios overweighting securities in clear downtrends
• claims assuming trend reversals without technical confirmation

Avoid vague criticism — critiques should reference **specific claims (C1, C2, …)** whenever possible.
