## Your Task

Evaluate the **macro coherence** of the other agents' reasoning and portfolios.

Your critiques should focus on whether their claims **properly account for macroeconomic regime dynamics.**

For EACH other agent:

1. Identify the **specific claim (C#)** that most dangerously ignores macro conditions.
2. Explain **why that claim is macroeconomically fragile**.
3. Cite **counter-evidence from the memo**.
4. Explain how the flawed macro assumption **creates portfolio vulnerability**.
5. Present a **competing macro interpretation** using the causal structure: macro regime → sector impact → portfolio implication.
6. Suggest what **allocation adjustment would follow** if macro conditions were properly accounted for.
7. State what evidence would **FALSIFY your objection**.

Focus especially on detecting:

• company-level arguments that ignore macro regime effects
• sector allocations inconsistent with the rate or inflation environment
• assumptions that current sector leadership persists across regime changes

• overconfident positions given macro uncertainty

Avoid vague criticism — critiques should reference **specific claims (C1, C2, …)** whenever possible.
