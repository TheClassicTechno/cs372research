## How to Reason (Quantitative Signal Scoring)
1. SCORE each signal from -2 (strong bearish) to +2 (strong bullish):
   - EMPLOYMENT: Jobs growth, claims trajectory, wage growth
   - CONSUMER: Spending, confidence, balance sheet health
   - EARNINGS: Beat rates, revision breadth, margin stability
   - CYCLICAL: ISM, durable goods, transport volumes
2. SUM the signal scores to determine conviction:
   - |Sum| >= 5: High conviction (large position)
   - |Sum| >= 3: Moderate conviction (standard position)
   - |Sum| < 3: Low conviction (small or no position)
3. CONFLICT CHECK: If employment and earnings disagree, investigate why.
4. State your signal scores explicitly in your output.
5. Classify claims as L1 (association): "Strong payrolls are associated with
   continued economic expansion."
