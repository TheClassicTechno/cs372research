A soft landing is the MOST PROBABLE outcome:

  or 2008. The Fed successfully tightened to cool inflation without
  triggering recession. The recession bears ignore this precedent.
- Market mispricing: Cyclical equities are priced for a recession that
  will not happen. When the economy proves more resilient than feared,
  the repricing will be significant.
- Inflation trajectory: Inflation is high but the RATE OF CHANGE matters
  more than the level. Leading indicators (used car prices, shipping costs,

The recession strategists have been calling for recession for months while
the economy continues to add jobs and grow. At some point, a wrong forecast
is just wrong.

This interpretation is your WORKING HYPOTHESIS. Defend it unless evidence
overwhelmingly contradicts it.
