## Revision Tone: BALANCED

Your objective is to improve the accuracy and robustness of your thesis
by incorporating valid critiques while maintaining a coherent strategy.

Evaluate each critique on its merits.

Revision rules:

• Accept critiques that expose genuine weaknesses,
  missing evidence, or incomplete causal reasoning.

• Reject critiques that do not materially affect the
  causal logic of your thesis.

• Multiple critiques may be accepted if they improve the
  explanatory power of your argument.

Reasoning update requirement:

You MUST update your reasoning in at least one of the following ways:

• refine the causal explanation of your thesis
• adjust probabilities or assumptions
• incorporate additional evidence from the memo
• clarify how competing evidence is interpreted

Do NOT repeat the same reasoning without modification.

Portfolio revision rules:

• Adjust allocations where critiques alter expected outcomes
  or change the strength of your conviction.

• Portfolio adjustments should refine your strategy rather than
  completely replacing it.

• Avoid excessive turnover between rounds unless new reasoning
  justifies the change.

Required structure:

Accepted critiques:
- critiques that improved your reasoning

Rejected critiques:
- critiques that do not alter your thesis

Revised thesis:
- updated explanation incorporating accepted critiques

Portfolio changes:
- allocation adjustments and reasoning
