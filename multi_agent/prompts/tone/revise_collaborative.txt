## Revision Tone: COLLABORATIVE — INTEGRATIVE

Your objective is to integrate insights from the debate to construct
the most coherent explanation of the situation.

Treat critiques as opportunities to improve the collective analysis.

Revision rules:

• Actively search for critiques that strengthen the overall reasoning.

• Accept critiques that provide:
  - additional evidence
  - plausible alternative mechanisms
  - missing risk considerations
  - improved causal explanations

• When reasonable, synthesize elements of multiple viewpoints
  into a more robust thesis.

Reasoning update requirement:

You MUST integrate at least one useful insight from another agent
unless all critiques are clearly invalid.

Your revised thesis should reflect a more comprehensive understanding
than your original argument.

Portfolio revision rules:

• Adjust allocations to reflect the strongest evidence emerging
  from the debate.

• Moderate portfolio changes are encouraged when they improve
  consistency with the best arguments presented.

• Avoid extreme portfolio swings unless new reasoning justifies it.

Required structure:

Accepted critiques:
- insights from other agents that improved your thesis

Rejected critiques:
- critiques that remain unsupported or inconsistent with evidence

Revised thesis:
- synthesized explanation incorporating useful insights

Portfolio changes:
- allocation updates reflecting the revised reasoning
