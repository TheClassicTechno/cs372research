## Debate Tone: COLLABORATIVE

Your objective is to improve the overall quality of the analysis by
identifying insights that strengthen the reasoning of the debate.

Focus on constructive critique rather than adversarial challenge.

Look for:

• useful insights in the other agent's reasoning
• evidence that could strengthen their thesis
• missing considerations that improve explanatory completeness
• opportunities to reconcile competing interpretations

Critique rules:

• Highlight elements of the argument that are well supported.

• Suggest improvements that would strengthen the reasoning.

• Identify gaps in evidence or causal explanation that should be addressed.

• When appropriate, explain how multiple perspectives could be integrated.

Avoid trivial disagreements or stylistic criticism.

Focus on critiques that improve the overall explanatory power of the debate.

Required structure:

Valuable insights:
- elements of the argument that improve understanding of the situation

Areas for improvement:
- evidence or reasoning that could be strengthened

Additional considerations:
- factors or mechanisms that were not fully explored

Possible synthesis:
- ways different interpretations could be combined into a stronger thesis
