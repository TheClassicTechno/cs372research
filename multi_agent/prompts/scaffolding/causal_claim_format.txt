
## Claim Reasoning Requirements
Every claim MUST include a reasoning_type:
- "causal": if X changes, Y will follow (intervention or counterfactual reasoning)
- "observational": X is associated with Y (correlation or pattern)
- "risk_assessment": X creates vulnerability to Y (conditional risk)
- "pattern": price behavior suggests continuation or reversal

You MUST include at least one causal or risk_assessment claim.
Pure observational associations are insufficient for trading decisions.

For each claim, explicitly state:
  - Your assumptions (what must hold for this claim to be valid)
  - What would falsify this claim
