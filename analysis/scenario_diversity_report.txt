  OVERALL VERDICT
    The 35 scenarios provide a reasonably diverse testbed for ablation
    experiments. They span 4+ years of market history including bull,
    bear, crisis, and recovery regimes. Portfolio sizes range from
    concentrated (12 tickers) to broad (35 tickers), and the
    36-ticker universe covers 7 sectors. Pairwise Jaccard overlap
    (mean=0.216) indicates meaningful differentiation between scenarios.

  CAVEATS / LIMITATIONS
    - Temporal coverage skews toward 2022 (11 scenarios) vs 2024 (2 scenarios)
    - Full-universe scenarios share the same ticker set, inflating some Jaccard pairs
    - No emerging-market, small-cap, or fixed-income exposure
    - All scenarios use the same constraint structure (fully_invested + cash virtual ticker)
