7. WHY ABLATION 8 WAS NULL — SYNTHESIS
================================================================================

HYPOTHESIS 1: THE THIRD AGENT DILUTES THE JUDGE'S SIGNAL (PRIMARY)

With 2 agents (ablation 7), preserving diversity means the judge hears
TWO genuinely different allocation philosophies. The judge can triangulate
between a macro view and a technical view, and the diversity itself is
informative.

With 3 agents (ablation 8), preserving diversity means the judge hears
THREE views. But the risk agent's perspective is inherently DEFENSIVE
— it biases toward lower exposure, higher cash, tail-risk hedging. When
the intervention forces ALL THREE agents to maintain independent views,
the risk agent's persistent caution may cancel out any alpha signal from
preserved macro-technical disagreement.

Evidence: Ablation 8's baseline already has lower absolute returns
(a_mean excess return: -2.06% vs -1.73% in ablation 7), suggesting the
risk agent's presence systematically pulls the judge toward more
conservative portfolios with lower alpha potential.

HYPOTHESIS 2: NEAR-UNIVERSAL FIRING WASHES OUT THE SIGNAL

In ablation 7, the intervention fires in 60% of treatment runs. This
means 40% of treatment runs are IDENTICAL to baseline (no intervention
needed). The signal comes from the 60% where the nudge matters. With
14 "free" pairs (where treatment = baseline) and 21 "treated" pairs,
the paired test has a clean contrast.

In ablation 8, the intervention fires in 97% of treatment runs (34/35).
This means every pair is "treated." But the effect is uniform and small
per-scenario — the nudge bumps the collapse ratio from ~0.50 to ~0.78
everywhere, creating a floor effect. The financial impact of this uniform
nudge may be noise-dominated because every scenario gets the same
generic "maintain disagreement" push, regardless of whether disagreement
was informative for that particular scenario.

HYPOTHESIS 3: 3-AGENT COLLAPSE IS STRUCTURALLY DIFFERENT

With 2 agents, collapse means they converged to a single view. The
intervention recovers genuine diversity.

With 3 agents, the baseline collapse ratio is 0.50 (much lower than
0.70 in ablation 7). This SEVERE collapse means the 3-agent revision
process systematically homogenizes views. The intervention bumps this
to 0.78, but the resulting diversity may be ARTIFICIAL — agents forced
to maintain positions they've already been "convinced" to abandon. This
creates shallow disagreement (different numbers) without deep
disagreement (different investment theses).

Evidence: The CRIT scores show that treatment in ablation 8 DEGRADES
reasoning quality across all agents and pillars. The nudge forces agents
to disagree, but the disagreement is low-quality (more contradictions,
more conclusion drift, less alternative consideration).

HYPOTHESIS 4: REASONING DEGRADATION OFFSETS DIVERSITY BENEFIT

The intervention in ablation 8 causes reasoning quality to decline
(Alternative Consideration drops for all agents, conclusion_drift
increases). In ablation 7, this degradation was minimal. The net effect
in ablation 8 may be: the judge receives more diverse BUT lower quality
inputs, and the quality degradation cancels the diversity benefit.

HYPOTHESIS 5: VOLATILITY AND VARIANCE SWAMPING

Ablation 8 has LOWER baseline volatility (16.56% vs 20.44% in ablation 7)
and lower max drawdown (8.07% vs 10.03%). The 3-agent system with the
risk agent produces inherently calmer portfolios. With less financial
variance, the signal-to-noise ratio for detecting treatment effects is
worse, and modest alpha improvements drown in noise.

Evidence: The SEM for excess return is 0.87/0.94 in ablation 8 vs
1.03/1.08 in ablation 7. Despite lower variance, the effect size
collapsed more dramatically (0.83 -> 0.16), suggesting the real issue
is effect size, not statistical power.

9. BOTTOM LINE
================================================================================

Ablation 8 demonstrates that the JS-collapse intervention scales
MECHANICALLY (higher firing rate, larger collapse-ratio recovery) but
NOT FINANCIALLY when a third agent is added. The most likely explanation
is a combination of:

  (a) The risk agent's inherent conservatism dilutes the alpha signal
      that diversity preservation generates in a 2-agent system.

  (b) Near-universal intervention firing (97%) creates a uniform
      treatment that lacks the selective targeting of ablation 7's 60%
      firing rate.

  (c) The nudge prompts degrade reasoning quality in the 3-agent setup
      (more contradictions, conclusion drift, lower alternative
      consideration), offsetting any diversity benefit.

This result, combined with ablation 10's null financial result from
improving causal reasoning, suggests that the financial value of the
intervention system is SPECIFIC to the 2-agent macro-technical
configuration and the moderate intervention rate. More agents and more
frequent intervention do not improve outcomes.
